Passion Project

A passion project of mine is a table top game called Warhammer 40k. For this game you buy the pieces which are unassembled and unpainted, and in order to use them you have to put them together which allows some flexibility in how you do so. Kind of a mix and match sort of thing to customize your army. Once you have the pieces put together, you paint them and you really get what you put into it here. You have total contol over the appearance of your army. Once you are ready to play the game is when the logical thinking comes in. The game is very strategic and you need to try to plan ahead as many moves as you can, and one bad turn can change the tides of the game. Overall it is a multi-faceted hobby that is a lot of fun.
